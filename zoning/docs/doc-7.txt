 ORDINANCE

BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF CHICAGO:
SECTION 1. Title 17 of the Municipal Code of Chicago, the Chicago Zoning Ordinance, is
hereby amended by changing all the B3-1 Community Shopping District symbols and indications
as shown on Map No. 11 -L in the area bounded by
North Linder Avenue; West Montrose Avenue; a line 302.05 feet east of and
parallel to North Linder Avenue; and the alley immediately south of and parallel
to West Montrose Avenue
to those of an RT-4 Two Flat, TowTihouse and Multi-Unit District and a corresponding use
district is hereby established in the area above described.
SECTION 2. This ordinance shall be in force and effect from and after its passage and due
publication.

4355 North Linder Avenue (4347-59 North Linder Avenue;
and 5431-59 West Montrose Avenue)
