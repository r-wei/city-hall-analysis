 ORDINANCE

BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CITY COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF CHICAGO:

SECTION 1. Title 17 of the Municipal Code of Chicago, the Chicago Zoning Ordinance,
is hereby amended by changing all ofthe B3-3 Community Shopping District symbols and
indications as shown on Map No. 7-F in the area bounded by
North Clark Street; a line 106.88 feet south of (as measured along North Clark
Street) and parallel to West Wellington Avenue; the public alley next east of
and parallel to North Clark Street; and a line 203.71 feet south of (as measured
along North Clark Street) and parallel to West Wellington Avenue,

to those ofa B3-2 Community Shopping District.

SECTION 2. This ordinance takes effect after its passage and approval.
