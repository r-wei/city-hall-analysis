Dear Ms. Mendoza:

I hereby call a special meeting of the City Council of the City of Chicago, to be convened
at 10:00 a.m. on Tuesday, September 22, 2015, in the City Council Chamber in City Hall, for the
following purposes and for no other purpose whatsoever:
1.

To receive a supplemental appropriation for fiscal year 2015; and

2.

To receive the Executive Budget for the year beginning January 1, 2016, and
ending December 31, 2016, and the Mayor's Budget Message relating thereto; and

3.

To receive the Year XLII (42) Community Development Block Grant
recommendations; and

4.

To receive and place on file a communication from the Budget Director regarding
estimated levies; and

5.

To consider a resolution calling for publication of the Executive Budget and
setting the date, time and place of the public hearing on the Executive Budget.
Very truly yours.

Mayor
